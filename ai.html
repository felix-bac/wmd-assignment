A.I Revolutionizes Healthcare, Saving Lives worldwide

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the healthcare industry, emerging as an effective ally in the fight to save lives. From disease detection at an early stage in the curriculum to targeted treatments and rescue during disasters, AI-based technologies are enabling faster and more accurate delivery of medical interventions, improving patient outcomes, and addressing world health issues. With hospitals, researchers, and governments embracing these technologies, AI is emerging as a game-saver when it comes to offering life-saving treatment.

Global Electricity Demand Surges, Ushering in a New Energy-Hungry Era

The world is experiencing a record increase in electricity demand driven by rapid industrialization, transportation electrification, and unabated expansion of data centers and artificial intelligence (AI).

DeepSeek’s Rise to Fame: Redefining AI Innovation Globally

In January 2025, a relatively unknown Chinese AI startup, DeepSeek, stunned the global tech industry with the release of its R1 model, a large language model (LLM) that rivals the performance of industry giants like OpenAI’s ChatGPT at a fraction of the cost. DeepSeek's debut has been meteoric, spurring a price war, shocking markets, and rocking the world of artificial intelligence, a "black swan" of tech. DeepSeek unlocks a new generation of low-cost, high-performance artificial intelligence, from car integrations to privacy concerns.

Inside the Black Box: How are A.I models Trained

Artificial Intelligence (AI) models, from chatbots like Grok to image creators like DALL-E, are ubiquitous now, but training them is a mysterious and clandestine art. They need massive amounts of data, massive computing powers, and sophisticated algorithms to transform raw data into programs that can think, write, or recognize images.

Will AI Ever Take Over the World? Experts Weigh In

The notion of artificial intelligence (AI) seizing control of the world has long fueled science fiction, from The Terminator to Ex Machina. But as AI systems like DeepSeek’s R1, xAI’s Grok 3, and OpenAI’s ChatGPT grow increasingly sophisticated, the question persists: Could AI ever dominate humanity? Experts in technology, ethics, and policy are grappling with this debate, weighing AI’s potential against its limitations and the safeguards shaping its future.
